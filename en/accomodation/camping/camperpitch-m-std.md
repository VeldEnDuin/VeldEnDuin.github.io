These pitches are suitable for those travelling with a motorhome or caravan up to 7.5 metres and looking for a quiet spot.

Pitch A34 is located at the front of the campsite, on a lawn with rental chalets and hiker pitches, opposite a modern small sanitary block.
Pitch C42 is a spacious pitch and is very quiet, located between the residential caravans and a little further from the large sanitary block.



## Worth knowing

- suitable for mobile homes and caravans up to 7,5 metres
- max 6 persons / pitch
- winter period 15/11-15/03: closed


## Facilities

- electricity: 10 amperes
- Belgian type E plug with earth pin
- water connection on the pitch
- drainage connection on the pitch (own hose needed)
- chemical toilet disposal in sanitary building
