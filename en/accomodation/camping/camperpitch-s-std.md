Our compact camping pitches are suitable for those traveling with a motorhome or small caravan up to 6.5 meters.

Awnings cannot be erected on these pitches, and they are not suitable for tents or trailer tents.

These paved pitches are located between the residential caravans, creating a pleasant and sheltered atmosphere. Although these pitches are slightly smaller and further away from the sanitary block, they offer everything you need for a comfortable stay by the sea.


## Worth knowing

- suitable for mobile homes and caravans up to 6,5 metres
- max 6 persons / pitch
- winter period 15/11-15/03: closed


## Facilities

- electricity: 10 amperes
- Belgian type E plug with earth pin
- water connection on the pitch
- drainage connection on the pitch (own hose needed)
- chemical toilet disposal in sanitary building
