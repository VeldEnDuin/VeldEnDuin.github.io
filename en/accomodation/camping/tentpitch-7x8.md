# TENT PITCH 7x8

These pitches of 7x8 m are convenient for small (self-constructing) tents.   


## Worth knowing

- max 4 persons/ pitch
- dogs allowed - max 2 – no dangerous breeds
- not accessible between 15/01 - 01/03 and 15/11 - 15/12
